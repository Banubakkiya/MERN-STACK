HISTORY

KFC, a subsidiary of Yum! Brands, Inc., is a global chicken restaurant brand. Today KFC India's 1300+ restaurants across 240+ cities, serve a variety of signature menu items from the iconic Hot & Crispy Chicken, fiery Hot Wings, chicken popcorn, flavourful Zinger Burger, aromatic Rice Bowlz, juicy Boneless Chicken Strips, and wholesome Family Feasts. Discover KFC's range of Value Meals, Veg Zinger, French Fries, and refreshing beverages. The address of this restaurant is Chennai Central Railway Station, EVR Road, Park Town, Chennai, Tamil Nadu.

MISSION

KFC has the widest global footprint of any quick-service restaurant brand, and we have no plans to slow down. We see big growth opportunities in many markets across the world and strive to be the QSR in every market where we operate.
